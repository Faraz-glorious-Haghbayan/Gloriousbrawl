About me

Welcome to my Brawl Stars fan website! I’m Faraz Haghbayan, a 16-year-old front-end developer from Iran, living in Germany, who’s passionate about web development and Brawl Stars. I've been playing Brawl Stars for many years and decided to create this site as a way to combine my love for the game with my interest in coding.

This project is more than just a hobby – it's an opportunity for me to learn, grow, and refine my web development skills. While the primary goal has never been about making money, I do appreciate the support through ads that help me cover some of the costs and motivate me to keep improving.

Thank you for visiting, and I hope you enjoy the website as much as I enjoyed building it!
